& i'm certainly someone who fights for equality.

I love Computer Science but that does not mean it is an issue-free field. In fact, A National Public Radio report in 2013 stated that about 20% of all U.S. computer programmers are female. In open source fields, only 10% of women are programmers. Looking around in my classes, I have seen this hasn't changed much in the past 6 years. I have worked hard against this in all of my jobs as well as in the organizations I take part in (ACM-Women in Computing and Kappa Eta Kappa: a professional co-ed CS and Engineering Fraternity). This poster is one I created in Photoshop for a school project that reflects my passion for getting women involved in CS.
